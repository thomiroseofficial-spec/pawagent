Olive: Healthy Food Scanner - Premium Yuka Alternative & Ingredient Checker. Your Personal Guide to Eating Healthy Looking for a food scanner that helps you eat healthy, avoid toxic additives, and find cleaner alternatives? Olive is the all-in-one healthy food scanner, ingredient checker, and food checker app that makes grocery shopping stress-free. Instantly scan barcodes to reveal whether a product contains gluten, synthetic additives, allergens, toxins, or hidden chemicals. Whether you’re vegan, managing food allergies, or just trying to avoid seed oils and processed sugar, Olive empowers you with transparency, knowledge, and healthier choices for you and your family. ◆ FEATURES OF OUR HEALTHY FOOD SCANNER* 1. Instant Food Scanner – Scan any barcode to check for harmful additives, seed oils, gluten, pesticides, toxins, or synthetic ingredients. 2. Ingredient Checker – Break down confusing food labels into clear insights. Easily spot hidden sugars, artificial colors, preservatives, and allergens. 3. Healthy Alternatives – Get personalized product suggestions that align with your lifestyle, whether you’re vegan, gluten-free, or allergy-sensitive. 4. Food Checker for Restaurants – Discover seed oil–free restaurants near you for cleaner, worry-free dining. 5. Lab-Tested Transparency – Go beyond surface-level info. Olive includes recall alerts, contaminant reports (PFAS, microplastics, heavy metals), and lab-tested data. 6. Community & Database – Access over 1 million products, with new items added daily thanks to a growing health-conscious community. *features listed are accessible through the Olive Premium subscription ◆ BENEFITS OF USING A HEALTHY FOOD SCANNER APP * Eat with confidence knowing what’s really in your food. * Reduce exposure to harmful toxins, additives, and synthetic chemicals. * Support your family’s health and boost energy, digestion, and immunity. * Enjoy peace of mind dining out with our seed oil–free restaurant finder. ◆ WHY CHOOSE OLIVE OVER OTHER FOOD CHECKER APPS? Unlike basic food scanner apps, Olive combines ingredient checking, toxin detection, and unbiased healthy alternatives. Many apps stop at flagging products, but Olive goes further: * Unbiased results – No ads, no brand influence, no hidden sponsorships. * Lab-tested reliability – Backed by scientific analysis and recall alerts. * Holistic scope – Scan food, water, and cosmetics (coming soon) for harmful substances. ◆ HOW THE OLIVE FOOD SCANNER WORKS (3 SIMPLE STEPS) * Scan – Point your camera at any food, drink, or household product barcode. * Analyze – Instantly see a breakdown of ingredients, allergens, and toxins. * Choose Better – Get safe, healthier alternatives tailored to your dietary needs. ◆ FAQS ABOUT THE OLIVE HEALTHY FOOD SCANNER What is Olive? Olive is a holistic healthy food scanner and ingredient checker app that helps you quickly scan food labels, detect toxins, and find cleaner alternatives. Is Olive good for allergies? Yes! Olive highlights allergens such as gluten, dairy, soy, and nuts—making it ideal for families with food sensitivities. Does Olive only scan food? No. Olive also checks bottled water for contaminants like fluoride and heavy metals, and soon you’ll be able to scan cosmetics and personal care products Why is Olive the best food scanner? Unlike other food checker apps, Olive uses lab-tested data, unbiased ratings, and a seed oil–free restaurant map to give you more transparency and healthier choices. Join the hundreds of thousands switching from Yuka, Bobby Approved, Seed Oil Scout and other food scanner apps to Olive. Download today for safer, healthier grocery shopping and toxin-free living!   • Great but some issues

    08/30/2025

    Frank275

    Ok so this app is amazing! It very easily scans any product and pretty much breaks it down and easy to read and understand. If you bump into a product that has no data, the app will tell you to take a picture of the front of it and ingredients and add it to the database! So why no 5 stars? I gave it 4 stars because the app has one major issue, there are several items that are the same item but have different scores. For example Sparkling Ice has 3 different scores where it’s a 0, 24, and 100. The apps doesn’t tell you if the differences in the score is due to different flavor or a different type of category like water vs lemonade. Another example would be Zevia soda. When I scan it the app tells me it’s a 63 but the alternative the app gives me is a 100 and the two products are the same. Other than that the app is amazing!

    Developer Response

    Hey Frank! Thanks so much for this detailed feedback. I totally hear where you're coming from. Sometimes, the recommended products have incorrect data, meaning that they might show a high score and the ingredients are actually wrong. We are actively improving this and updating products to make sure we have the most accurate data, and users can also correct products that have incorrect ingredients. The other thing that could be going on is that some products from the same brand in the same category (like different flavors) have different ingredients which affect the score differently. We really appreciate you using Olive, and if you ever have any more feedback or concerns, I'd be happy to chat over the phone or email! You can reach me directly at support@oliveapp.com.
